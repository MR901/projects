### PX100 (Degumming) – Typical Operating Notes

| Item | Normal | Alarm | Trip |
|------|--------|-------|------|
| Vibration (mm s⁻¹ RMS) | < 3 | ≥ 5 | ≥ 7 |
| Upper bearing temperature (°C) | < 75 | ≥ 80 | ≥ 90 |

* **Sludge imbalance trend** – After ~12 h run time the 1× RPM peak can grow from 2→6 mm s⁻¹; schedule partial bowl cleaning before 6 mm s⁻¹.  
* **Needle-bearing hotspot** – Upper bearing overheats first; watch PT100_upper ≥ 80 °C.  
* **Feed discipline** – Keep oil at 70 ± 5 °C; cold feed → rapid viscosity jump → vibration spike.  
* **Hydraulic ejection** every 5–20 min; verify operating-water pressure 0.4–0.6 MPa.
