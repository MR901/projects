### RSB200 – Field Notes

* **Self-cleaning bowl** with double centripetal pump; desludge window adjustable 0.2–0.5 s.  
* **Typical issues**  
  * Premature solids ejection ↔ low operating-water pressure (<0.35 MPa).  
  * Bowl-seal O-ring swell (nitrile) after > 18 months; vibration at 0.31× RPM sideband.  
* **Recommended checks**  
  1. Verify bowl-speed sensor (proximity switch, manual p. 7) → 4050 ± 30 rpm.  
  2. Bump-test to confirm natural frequency 510 Hz (if > 520 Hz suspect stiff build-up).
